Every publication below is written in markdown or Typst in the open, compiled to a branded, accessible (PDF/UA-1) PDF, and cites the same public archives that back the rest of this site — so any figure or number can be traced to its source and reproduced. Source files live in the [publications repository](https://github.com/sustainable-fsa/publications). A companion analysis — projecting how grazing periods are likely to shift under future climate using NASA's downscaled CMIP6 ensemble — is complete and being prepared for publication; it will appear here once the manuscript is submitted.

Alongside these, four **University of Montana M.S. theses** examine, through interviews and field research, how FSA's livestock disaster programs are administered on the ground and how producers experience them. Two were directly supported by this project; two were advised by the PI and extend the project's lens to drought planning and water scarcity in adjacent regions. The [Team page](/team/) names the researchers and their faculty committee chairs.
